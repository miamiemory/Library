Printing Documents from Anywhere!

Need to print a document? With our remote printing services, you can email your files to the library from anywhere and our staff will print them for you and have them ready at the door for pickup.


Printing cost is $0.20 per page (black & white or color)


Faxing available at all branches except Randleman. Cost is $1.75 for the first page and $1.00 for each additional page.



Send your print jobs to the email to your desired pick-up location.
